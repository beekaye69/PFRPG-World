## COMBAT MAGIC (WIZARD EXCLUSIVE)

**Allocation:** 2 per encounter

**Spells Available:**
- Aid from Beyond (support reroll grant)
- Arcane Armor (defense buff)
- Confusion (crowd control)
- Dark Bolt (ranged damage)
- Rain of Fire (multi-target damage)
- Summon Monster (action economy)

### Spell Mechanics

Each spell:
- Costs 1 combat action
- Uses relevant d6 roll (usually CS-based)
- Counts toward 2 per encounter limit
- Can be used in any order

### Strategic Casting

**Offensive Encounter:** Dark Bolt + Rain of Fire
**Defensive Encounter:** Arcane Armor + Confusion
**Support Encounter:** Aid from Beyond + Arcane Armor
**Unknown:** Dark Bolt + Aid (baseline flexible)

## FREE FORM MAGIC (WIZARD EXCLUSIVE)

**Allocation:** 2 per session

**System:**
- Out-of-combat only
- Player proposes effect
- PM determines difficulty
- Success: Effect happens
- Failure: Slot wasted

### Free Form Applications

| Type | Example | Difficulty |
|------|---------|-----------|
| Creation | Create bridge | Average (4+) |
| Conjure | Food/water | Simple (3+) |
| Detection | Find secrets | Tough (5+) |
| Transform | Change appearance | Very Diff (6) |
| Communication | Animal talk | Average (4+) |
| Levitation | Short flight | Tough (5+) |

## BARDIC MAGIC (BARD EXCLUSIVE)

**System:** 7 different songs, sing continuously for 3+ rounds

**Songs:**
1. Hymn of Mighty (+1 damage all party)
2. Lay of Deflection (+1 defense all party)
3. Ballad of Legends (Heroic Reroll on 3+)
4. Song of Mending (Heal 1 hp/round all party)
5. Chant of Renewal (Full heal, one-time)
6. Melody of Madness (Confuse enemies)
7. Shanty of Pain (Damage reflection)

**Mechanics:**
- No action cost after initial setup
- Bard can act normally while singing
- Duration: 3+ rounds
- Effect: Continuous until combat ends or song stops

### Song Selection Strategy

| Encounter Type | Best Song | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Offense-heavy | Hymn of Mighty | Amplify party damage |
| Defense-heavy | Lay of Deflection | Reduce incoming damage |
| Long fight | Ballad of Legends | Generate resources |
| Healing needed | Song of Mending | Sustain party |
| Emergency | Chant of Renewal | Full party recovery |
| Area threat | Melody of Madness | Area control |
| Multi-hitter | Shanty of Pain | Punish multi-attackers |

## MAGIC INTERACTION WITH SYSTEMS

**With Core Mechanics:**
- Spell rolls use d6 system
- Explosion possible on spell damage (natural 6)
- Heroic Reroll usable on spell rolls

**With Combat:**
- Spell = 1 action per turn
- Counts against 2 spells per encounter
- Can cast from distance (ranged safety)

**With Items:**
- Magical items can grant spell access
- Staff can hold multiple spell slots
- Wand can focus single spell
