# PFRPG MASS COMBAT: TACTICAL DECISION TREE
## Logic Gates for Battlefield Adjudication

---

## GATE 1: THE MOVEMENT PHASE
- **Standard Move:** 1 Unit of distance (approx. 100 yards).
- **Forced March:** Make a **Strength Task Roll (4+)**. Success = Move 2 Units. Failure = Lose 1 HP (Fatigue).

## GATE 2: TERRAIN INTERACTIONS
| Terrain | Unit Type | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Woods** | `[Archers]` | Range reduced to 1 Unit. |
| **Woods** | `[Cavalry]` | CS Roll with Disadvantage (d6-2). |
| **Mud/Swamp** | `[Heavy]` | Movement reduced to 0.5 Units. |
| **High Ground**| **Any** | +1 to CS Roll vs. units below. |

## GATE 3: THE "FOG OF WAR"
- **Scouting:** A `[Thief]` or `[Ranger]` leads a scout party.
- **Mechanic:** **Intelligence Task Roll (Tough 5+)**.
- **Success:** Enemy unit positions and Tags are revealed to the Commander.
- **Failure:** The scout party is ambushed (Take d6 damage).
