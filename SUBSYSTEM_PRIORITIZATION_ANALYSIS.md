## üéØ ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK

**Scoring Criteria (1-5 scale):**
- **Complexity:** How many moving parts? (1=simple, 5=complex)
- **Decision Points:** How many choices must be made? (1=linear, 5=highly branching)
- **Cross-System Impact:** How much affects other systems? (1=isolated, 5=central hub)
- **User Confusion Risk:** How likely to be misunderstood? (1=clear, 5=very confusing)
- **Practical Utility:** How often will this be used? (1=rare, 5=constant)
- **Example Effectiveness:** How much do examples help? (1=unhelpful, 5=critical)

**Score Interpretation:**
- **25-30:** IMMEDIATE PRIORITY (create immediately after combat)
- **20-24:** HIGH PRIORITY (create in next batch)
- **15-19:** MEDIUM PRIORITY (helpful but not critical)
- **10-14:** LOW PRIORITY (good reference but low confusion risk)
- **5-9:** MINIMAL PRIORITY (clear as-is)
