# PFRPG MASS COMBAT: LOGISTICS & ECONOMY
## System: v4.2 | Derived from Rules Cyclopedia Pay Scales

---

## SECTION 1: MAINTENANCE (MONTHLY)
Army costs are paid in Gold Pieces (gp).

- **Standard Pay:** 1gp per HP per Month (e.g., 100hp Army = 100gp/mo).
- **Elite/Veteran Pay:** 2gp per HP per Month.
- **Mercenary Premium:** Double the base pay (requires upfront contract).

## SECTION 2: THE SUPPLY LINE
An army travels with a supply train. If cut off, the army faces attrition.
- **Foraging:** The Commander makes a **Wisdom Task Roll (Average 4+)**.
  - **Success:** Army is fed for d6 days.
  - **Failure:** Army loses 1 Unit HP per day until fed.

## SECTION 3: CASUALTY RECOVERY (RE-MANNING)
After a battle, units can recover lost HP (wounded returning to duty).
- **Standard Recovery:** Unit recovers 1 HP per week of rest.
- **Cleric Intervention:** If a `[Cleric]` uses a Spell Action, the unit recovers 1d6 HP immediately post-battle.
- **Recruitment:** Refilling a unit costs 5gp per 1 HP (cost of gear/recruitment).
