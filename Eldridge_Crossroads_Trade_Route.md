### The East Road - Route Profile

**Total Distance:** 2 miles (roughly 0.5-0.75 hours wagon travel at normal pace)  
**Travel Type:** Well-maintained merchant road, stone-paved sections, muddy during rain  
**Terrain:** Gently rolling pastoral farmland, three major water features, one significant ravine  
**Settlement Pattern:** Scattered farms and homesteads along route, but no major population centers  
**Safety:** Generally safe during daylight; militia patrol regularly; post-goblin-raid vigilance increased

**Population Distribution:**
- Eldridge (starting point): ~200
- Route farmsteads: ~40-50 (scattered across multiple properties)
- Crossroads (destination): ~60
- Total regional: ~300-310

### SEGMENT 1: ELDRIDGE SOUTH EDGE TO MID-ROUTE (0.0 - 1.0 miles)

#### Starting Point: Eldridge South Gate

**Visual Description:**
Leaving Eldridge southward, you pass the grain warehouses (economic heartbeat still visible behind you). The town diminishes as you move away from the central market district. Stone-paved road gives way to packed earth (still well-maintained, but less urban). Fields begin immediately south of town edge.

**Sensory Details:**
- Smell: Shift from town-woodsmoke to open-field air (fresher, grain-scented still from local production)
- Sound: Town noise fades; replaced by bird calls, wind through fields, occasional livestock
- Sight: Agricultural landscape opens before you; horizon expands

**Road Condition:**
Solid packed earth, barely muddy even after rain (good drainage). Wagon ruts visible (regular merchant traffic). Marginally overgrown on sides (farmers keep center clear, edges grow wild).

#### LOCATION: CROSSFIELD CREEK BRIDGE (0.4 miles from Eldridge)

**Type:** Stone bridge crossing, original medieval construction, well-maintained  
**Status:** Undamaged (contrast with later ravine crossing)  
**Strategic Importance:** Primary water source crossing, reliable structure

**Visual Description:**
The road narrows slightly as it approaches the bridge (intentional‚Äîfocus traffic). The creek flows beneath (roughly 20 feet across, 6-8 feet deep, swift water during rain, normal flow otherwise). The bridge is solid stone construction‚Äîfive arches, weathered but strong. It's clearly ancient (original medieval construction, perhaps 300+ years old).

**Bridge Details:**
- Material: Cut stone (gray granite-like)
- Width: Roughly 15 feet (wagon-width plus margin)
- Height above water: 10-12 feet (above normal water level, floods don't reach it)
- Condition: Excellent (regularly maintained, militia responsibility)
- Passage: Safe for all vehicles, no hazard

**Sensory Details:**
- Sight: Stone bridge elegant but utilitarian; creek flowing beneath (clear water, visible rocks)
- Sound: Water rushing beneath (peaceful, rhythmic), echoes under bridge (crossing creates slight echo)
- Smell: Fresh water (creek scent, clean and cool), stone, slightly damp air near bridge
- Temperature: Noticeably cooler (water thermal effect, shade from bridge)

**Crossing Time:** 2-3 minutes (normal walking pace)

**Environmental Details:**
The creek runs perpendicular to road, flowing east-west (water sources the region). Trees line the creek (willows, alders, natural vegetation). The bank is grassy (good for rest, water access). No settlements directly at bridge (too close to both Eldridge and Crossroads for development).

**Encounter Possibility:**
- **No encounters typically** (bridge is safe, regular passage)
- **Occasional:** Fisherman or forager from nearby farm gathering water
- **Environmental:** Stream crossing during high water (seasonal, after heavy rain)

### SEGMENT 2: MID-ROUTE TO RAVINE CROSSING (1.0 - 1.5 miles)

#### LOCATION: BLACKSTONE RAVINE & GOBLIN INCURSION POINT (1.2 miles from Eldridge)

**Type:** Deep natural ravine, goblin emergence point, recent battle site, ongoing concern  
**Strategic Importance:** Major defensive concern, goblin territory marker  
**Recent Activity:** Scene of initial goblin raids, goblin scout encounters post-raid  
**Status:** Actively monitored by militia, known danger point

**Ravine Formation:**
The land slopes gradually south, then suddenly drops. A gorge opens beneath (roughly 50-70 feet deep, 40-50 feet wide). The ravine runs roughly east-west (perpendicular to the road). Sheer stone walls in places, sloped earth in others. A creek runs at the bottom (Blackstone Creek, dark water, rushing).

**Topography:**
- **Northern edge (merchant road):** Stone-paved road approaches ravine carefully
- **Ravine walls:** Mix of sheer stone (gray-black, hence "Blackstone"), loose earth, some vegetation
- **Bottom:** Stream flowing, rocks, some flat areas, cave openings visible in walls
- **Southern edge:** Stone-paved road resumes after careful descent

**The Bridge (Goblin-Damaged Structure):**
Originally a substantial stone bridge crossing the ravine (medieval construction, similar to Crossfield Creek). Recent goblin raids inflicted significant damage‚Äîstones displaced, one major arch partially collapsed, repair work ongoing.

**Bridge Status:**
- Current: Usable but damaged (passable for careful travel, wagon traffic slower)
- Repairs: Militia and volunteers patching damage (visible work, scaffolding)
- Safety: Safe enough but requires caution; Quartermaster has issued travel warnings
- Timeline: Expected full repair in 1-2 weeks

**Visual Description:**
The road approaches the ravine‚Äîyou notice the change in ground level first (slight warning). Then the chasm opens suddenly. Stone bridge spans it‚Äîimpressive engineering, but clearly *hurt*. The northern arch is visibly displaced (large stones out of alignment). Repair work is active (militia oversight, volunteer labor). Beyond the bridge, the road continues south into higher ground.

**Goblin Evidence:**
The ravine shows signs of goblin habitation‚Äîdisturbed soil, crude marks, discarded materials. The cave openings in the walls are where goblins nested. Stone markings visible (ritual scars observed on surfaces, goblin markings). It's clear this is *their* territory.

**Sensory Details:**

**Sight:**
- Damaged bridge (impressive but wounded)
- Deep ravine (dramatic drop)
- Dark water (Blackstone Creek, ominous-named)
- Goblin signs (crude markings, disturbances)
- Cave openings (dark, mysterious)
- Repair work (militia presence, ongoing labor)

**Sound:**
- Water rushing below (constant, loud in narrow ravine)
- Wind through gap (echoing, sometimes eerie)
- Repair work (hammer strikes, tool sounds)
- Occasional bird calls (ravens, cliff-dwelling birds)
- Militia communication (guards coordinating)

**Smell:**
- Water-dampness (creek moisture, stone-wet)
- Goblin presence (earthy, unpleasant‚Äîfamiliar from raid)
- Disturbed earth
- Militia presence (leather, oil, human)

**Temperature:**
Noticeably cooler (ravine acts as cool zone, stone thermal mass, water proximity). Wind is channeled through gap (can feel strong).

**Bridge Crossing:**
Normal passage time: 5-7 minutes careful pace (slower than normal bridge)  
Wagon passage: 10-15 minutes (requires careful navigation)  
Safety: No serious hazard if cautious (but requires attention)

**Militia Presence:**
- **Regular:** Guards posted (monitoring ravine, checking travelers)
- **Typical:** 2-4 militia at bridge during daylight
- **Night:** Reduced presence (dangerous after dark, travel discouraged)
- **Post-Raid:** Increased vigilance, more frequent patrols

**NPCs & Interaction:**

**Guard Captain Mareck** (Senior Militia at Bridge)
- Personality: Veteran, cautious, responsible for bridge security
- Role: Oversees repair work, checks travelers, maintains ravine monitoring
- Relationship to Gort: Knows of Gort's reputation, respectful, might brief on goblin activity
- Dialogue: "Hero, good to see you. Bridge'll be fixed soon. Goblins are still active‚Äîsaw signs yesterday. Watch the ravine crossing. Don't linger."

**Militia Laborers** (2-3 regularly)
- Role: Bridge repair work, under militia supervision
- Activity: Repairing damaged stones, reinforcing structure
- Interaction: Acknowledge travelers, continue work

**Goblin Monitoring:**
The ravine is under constant low-level surveillance (militia scouts check regularly). If Gort travels here, militia will acknowledge him and provide updates on goblin activity.

**Encounter Possibility:**
- **Regular:** Militia guards, bridge crossing, no danger
- **Possible:** Brief goblin scout encounter (rare, but militia warn of possibility)
- **Story Hook:** Militia might request reconnaissance into ravine (side quest potential)

**Strategic Notes:**
This location is the *boundary* between civilized trade route and goblin territory. It marks the frontier of safety. South of here, civilization becomes sparser. The ravine is the final major settlement area feature before the wilderness transition.
