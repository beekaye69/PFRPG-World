───────────┼─────────┼───────┼────────────────┼────────────────
SPRING     │ x1.2    │ x1.2  │ +10-15 min     │ Mud, streams
SUMMER     │ x1.0    │ x1.0  │ Normal         │ Heat (hydrate!)

WINTER     │ x1.5    │ x1.75 │ +20-30 min     │ Ice, snow,
           │         │       │ (possible ice) │ hypothermia

EXAMPLE: East Road travel times
  Spring: 40 min × 1.2 = 48 min walking
  Summer: 40 min × 1.0 = 40 min walking
  Autumn: 40 min × 1.0 = 40 min walking
  Winter: 40 min × 1.5 = 60 min walking (plus ice risk)

## QUICK QUESTION LOOKUP

```
Q: "How long to walk from Eldridge to Crossroads?"
A: 40-45 minutes (2.0 miles at ~3 mph walking pace)

Q: "What's at Blackstone Ravine?"
A: Goblin boundary, militia checkpoint, damaged bridge, hazard zone

Q: "Who sells medical supplies?"
A: Farmer Marta (Thornbush) or healer Lynda (Eldridge)

Q: "Fastest route to Kilhel?"
A: Eldridge → Crossroads (2 hr) → Kilhel (3.5-4 hrs) = 5.5-6 hrs total

Q: "What water sources on the route?"
A: Crossfield Creek Bridge, Wayfarers Rest, all farm wells

Q: "Are nights safe for travel?"
A: NO. Travel discouraged post-dark. Militia enforces dusk curfew near threats.

Q: "Wagon or walking—which is faster?"
A: Wagon slower (takes more time) but carries more. Walking is faster.

Q: "Best place to rest between settlements?"
A: Wayfarers Rest Shrine (midpoint) or any farmstead with hospitality

Q: "What direction is Tiny Stump from Eldridge?"
A: Northwest, 4.5 miles via Western Branch road

Q: "Any merchants on the route?"
A: Yes: Verdant Farm, Millbrook (Miller), all farms have goods
```

## COORDINATE SYSTEM REFERENCE

```
COORDINATE GRID (Eldridge as origin 0,0)

           NORTH (increasing +y)
              │
    WEST ────┼──── EAST
   (decreasing x)   (increasing +x)
              │
           SOUTH (decreasing -y)

KNOWN COORDINATES:
  Eldridge ........... (0, 0)      — ORIGIN
  Crossroads ......... (0, 2)      — North 2 miles
  Moonwood Tower ..... (0.3, 0.5)  — NE 1.2 miles
  Kilhel ............. (0, 4)      — North 4+ miles [estimate]
  Tiny Stump ......... (-1.5, 1.5) — NW 3.5+ miles [estimate]
  Goblin Hills ....... (0, -1.5)   — South 1.5+ miles
  Deepfang Cavern .... (0, -2)     — South 2+ miles

DISTANCE CALCULATION:
  √[(x₂-x₁)² + (y₂-y₁)²] × scaling factor
  Example: Eldridge (0,0) to Crossroads (0,2)
           = √[0 + 4] × 1.0 = 2.0 miles ✓
```
