## 4. SETTLEMENT MORALE TRACKING

### Morale Calculation
**Base:** 0.65 (neutral)  
**Modified by:**
- Recent violence/death: -0.10
- Recent victory/success: +0.10
- Economic crisis: -0.05 to -0.15
- Feast/celebration: +0.05
- NPC conflict escalation: -0.05
- Community cooperation: +0.05

### Morale Effects on Settlement
- **0.40 or lower:** Danger of settlement dissolution, emigrants leaving
- **0.40-0.60:** Cautious, some unrest, but functional
- **0.60-0.80:** Healthy, normal operations, cooperative
- **0.80+:** Excellent, high morale, increased productivity

### Daily Morale Roll
Each in-game week, roll 1d20:
- 1-5: Morale -0.03 (problems emerge)
- 6-15: Morale unchanged (stable)
- 16-20: Morale +0.03 (improvement)

## 5. COMBAT ENCOUNTER SCALING

### Party Level Adjustment

**Tiny Stump Goblin Assault (Default: 4-6 party members)**

**Party Level 1-2:**
- 15-20 goblin scouts + 3-4 goblin warriors
- Simple wall defense
- NPC combat support essential

**Party Level 3-4:**
- 25-35 goblins (mixed warriors/scouts) + 2-3 captains
- Complex wall defense with siege
- NPC support helpful but not essential

**Party Level 5+:**
- 40-50+ goblins + goblin leader + magic support
- Siege mechanics, flanking attempts
- Challenging even with support
- Potential for major NPC casualties

**Party Level 1-3 (Outnumbered Scenario):**
- Escape/defense focus rather than total victory
- Save key NPCs
- Establish defensible positions
- Call for military reinforcements
