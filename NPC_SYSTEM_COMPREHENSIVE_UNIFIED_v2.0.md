BEHAVIOR RESPONSE FRAMEWORK:

   - What are they absolutely terrified of?

   - Do they see a threat, opportunity, or neither?
   - What assumptions do they make based on appearance?
   - Would they respect, fear, pity, or dismiss the player?
   - Do the players remind them of someone?
   - What class/social standing do they assign?
