# SECTION 9: LOOT & TREASURE ATOMIZATION

## 9.1: Loot by Challenge
- Easy challenge (d6 per PC gold, 0 items, low value)
- Moderate challenge (d6 best per PC, maybe 1 item)
- Difficult challenge (d6x10 best per PC, 1-2 items, 40-60 gp)
- Boss challenge (d6x100 party total, d6 per PC items, 400-600 gp)

## 9.2: Gold Distribution
- Per-character split (each PC gets share)
- Party total split (divide total among PCs)
- Calculation examples
- Fair distribution principles

## 9.3: Item Drop Tables
- Boss kills: d6 roll per PC determines item
- 1-2: Nothing
- 3-4: Arcane Gem
- 5: Focus x1 or Deflect x1
- 6: Rare item or better

## 9.4: Gem Collection Strategy
- Early campaign (collect 3 for first x1)
- Mid campaign (accumulate 6-9 for x2 or second x1)
- Late campaign (maintain 15+ for multiple x3 items)

## 9.5: Loot Pacing & Player Satisfaction
- Too much loot (diminishes challenge)
- Too little loot (frustration)
- Optimal pacing (rewarding but not overwhelming)
- Rare item scarcity (keep special items special)
