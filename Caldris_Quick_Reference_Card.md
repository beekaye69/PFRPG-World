## ‚ùå COMMON MISTAKES (Avoid These)

**DON'T:**
- Assume Caldris is evil (he's not, just eccentric)
- Challenge his magical knowledge (he'll be very dismissive)
- Try sneaking into restricted areas (magical traps everywhere)
- Refuse to help with magical research (he's genuinely curious)
- Come to tower with obviously evil intent (gates seal)

**DO:**
- Show respect for his genius (he responds well to this)
- Ask questions about magic (he'll talk for hours)
- Prove your competence (he becomes interested in you)
- Be honest about your needs (he appreciates directness)
- Treat his staff well (they'll mention it to him)
