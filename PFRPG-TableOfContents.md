- Targets: Multiple allies possible

---

### 5.2.2 Arcane Armor Spell
**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#arcane-armor-spell

**Mechanics:**
- Target gains CS d6 defense rolls (not class-limited)
- Re-roll all defense, take best
- Duration: Entire encounter

**Strategic Use:** Defense buff, helps low-CS classes

---

### 5.2.3 Confusion Spell
**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#confusion-spell

**Mechanics:**
- Roll d6-3 (minimum 1) for duration
- Target cannot move or attack for duration
- Target can still defend

**Strategic Use:** Crowd control

---

### 5.2.4 Dark Bolt Spell
**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#dark-bolt-spell

**Mechanics:**
- CS d6 ranged attack
- May explode on natural 6
- Roll twice, take best

**Strategic Use:** Reliable ranged damage

---

### 5.2.5 Rain of Fire Spell
**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#rain-of-fire-spell

**Mechanics:**
- Target up to 4 enemies in close proximity
- CS d6-1 ranged attack
- Roll twice, take best
- Single roll applies to all targets

**Strategic Use:** Area-of-effect damage

---

### 5.2.6 Summon Monster Spell
**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#summon-monster-spell

**Mechanics:**
- Roll d6-2 (minimum 2) for duration in combat rounds
- Monster acts under caster control
- Monster doesn't consume caster action
- Monster options depend on rolls (Goblin ‚Üí Troll based on natural 6s)

**Strategic Use:** Action economy expansion, ally generation

---

### 5.3 Free Form Magic (Out-of-Combat Spells)
**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#free-form-magic-system

**Wizard Access:**
- 2 per session
- Out of combat only
- PM determines difficulty via task roll
- Failure = spell attempt wasted

**Typical Applications:**
- Conjure food/water
- Create items (boat, rope, bridge)
- Levitation
- Animal communication
- Secret door detection
- Telepathy
- Underwater breathing

**Strategic Use:** Problem-solving, exploration, creative solutions

### 6.1.1 Focus (Magical Weapons)
**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#focus-magical-weapons

**Mechanics:**
- Focus x1: Use once per encounter to deal 6 damage (no roll)
- Multiple Focus (x2, x3, etc.) = multiple uses
- Target still gets defense action
- Works with Fighter Combat Master ability (adds additional d6)

**Glossary References:**

### 7.1 Healing System

**Healing Types:**
1. Post-Encounter Healing (1 hp or 2 with Cleric)
2. Extended Rest (3 hp after 8 hours)
3. Magical healing (spells, items)
4. Potions (immediate use)

**Glossary References:**
- Healing [PFRPG-Glossary.md#healing-rest--death]

---

### 7.2 Rest Mechanics
**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#extended-rest-healing

**8-Hour Rest:**
- Heals 3 hp (not 1)
- Must be uninterrupted
- Maximum once per day
- Interruptions void healing

**Interruption Examples:**
- Combat
- Climbing
- Long distance travel
- Strenuous activity

**Glossary References:**
- Rest [PFRPG-Glossary.md#rest]

---

### 7.3 Death & Unconsciousness
**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#death--unconsciousness

**0 HP State:**
- Character becomes unconscious
- Must roll laughable task each round to avoid death
- Failed save = permanent death
- Continued success = another round of life

**Glossary References:**
- Death [PFRPG-Glossary.md#death]
- Unconscious [PFRPG-Glossary.md#unconscious]

---

### 7.4 Stabilization
**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#stabilization

**Stabilize Process:**
- Ally adjacent to 0 hp character
- Ally spends action
- Average task (4+) roll required
- Success = character stabilized

**Stabilized Effects:**
- Conscious but helpless
- Cannot move or act
- No death saves required
- Remains until healed to 1+ hp

**Glossary References:**
- Stabilize [PFRPG-Glossary.md#stabilize]

**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#monster-quantity-determination

### 8.2.6 Traps
**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#traps

**Trap Damage:** d6+ (no defense unless agility task succeeds)

**Trigger Conditions:**
- Opening containers: d6 roll of 1 = trapped
- Also in walls/ceiling/floors

**Trap Types:**
- Pits, spikes, darts, flames, gas, rocks, blades, custom

**Finding & Disarming:**
- Thief Nimble ability
- Wisdom task roll (active search)
- Secret door finding (wisdom/Nimble)

- Trap [PFRPG-Glossary.md#trap]

**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#room-features

**Multiple Features:** Can roll multiple times or choose related features

**Primary Source:** PFRPG-Analysis-Updated.md#bosses

**Boss Frequency:** One per dungeon level depth

**Boss Placement:** Near end of session or before deeper exploration

**Boss Selection:** Based on party combat efficiency

**Boss Stats:** CS d6, double hp or more (scale to party strength)

### 8.3.1 Easy Challenge Loot
- Gold: Each character finds d6 gp

---

### 8.3.2 Moderate Challenge Loot
- Gold: Each character finds d6 gp (re-roll, take best)
- Increased rewards

---

### 8.3.3 Difficult Challenge Loot
- Gold: Each character finds d6x10 gp (re-roll, take best)
- Enhanced rewards

---

### 8.3.4 Boss Challenge Loot
- Gold: Party finds d6x100 gp (re-roll, take best)
- Each character rolls d6:
  - 1-2: Nothing additional
  - 3-4: Arcane Gem
  - 5: Focus x1 weapon OR Deflect x1 armor (choice)
  - 6: Same as 5 OR Runic Forge (upgrade existing magic item)

**Runic Forge:** Upgrades Focus/Deflect by +1 (single use, magic exhausted)
