## OVERVIEW

Six playable character classes in PFRPG:
1. **Cleric** - Healer (8 hp, d6-1 CS)
2. **Fighter** - Tank/DPS (10 hp, d6 CS)
3. **Thief** - DPS/Scout (6 hp, d6-1 CS)
4. **Bard** - Support/Buffer (6 hp, d6-2 CS)
5. **Ranger** - Ranged/Scout (10 hp, d6-1 CS)
6. **Wizard** - Versatile/Control (4 hp, d6-2 CS)

---

## CLASS 1: CLERIC

**HP:** 8 | **CS:** d6-1

**Abilities:**
- Righteous Insight (Wisdom reroll)
- Bind Wounds (healing x2 per encounter)
- Holy Light or Smite (healing/damage spell)

**Role:** Healer + Support

**Mechanics:**
- Primary healing output (2 bind wounds per encounter + spell)
- Wisdom-based perception advantage
- Melee combat viable but not primary
- Post-encounter recovery (2 hp natural)

**Optimal Play:**
- Position defensively
- Manage healing resource (2 bind wounds)
- Use Righteous Insight on crucial checks
- Holy Light for healing, Smite for offense

**Tactical Tips:**
- Heal before parties reach critical
- Use perception advantage for trap detection
- Sit back from melee when possible

---

## CLASS 2: FIGHTER

**HP:** 10 | **CS:** d6

**Abilities:**
- Muscle (Strength reroll)
- Challenge (force enemy focus)
- Combat Master (additional d6 roll, explosion potential)

**Role:** Tank + DPS

**Mechanics:**
- Highest HP (tank role)
- Highest CS (best in combat)
- Combat Master explosions (6 → roll d6 → chain)
- Strength advantage for physical tasks

**Optimal Play:**
- Frontline melee fighter
- Use Combat Master on boss fights
- Use Challenge to protect weaker allies
- Leverage Muscle on physical tasks

**Tactical Tips:**
- Use Focus weapons to maximize Combat Master
- Concentrate fire with allies
- Challenge high-damage enemies to save allies

---

## CLASS 3: THIEF

**HP:** 6 | **CS:** d6-1

**Abilities:**
- Nimble (Agility reroll + lockpicking)
- Flank (dual attack rolls, take best)
- Backstab (double damage from hiding)

**Role:** DPS + Scout

**Mechanics:**
- Lower HP (fragile, needs positioning)
- Agility reroll for stealth/locks
- Flank doubles attack opportunity (dual rolls)
- Backstab is opener from hiding

**Optimal Play:**
- Scout ahead (use Nimble for stealth)
- Position for Flank (behind enemy while ally engages)
- Backstab at combat start if possible
- Avoid frontline melee

**Tactical Tips:**
- Use terrain to hide
- Flank with Fighter for maximum damage
- Backstab only at combat start (target unaware)

---

## CLASS 4: BARD

**HP:** 6 | **CS:** d6-2

**Abilities:**
- Charming (Charisma reroll)
- Loremaster (knowledge reroll)
- Bardic Music (7 songs, continuous support)

**Role:** Support + Buffer

**Mechanics:**
- Lowest CS (weak in combat)
- Fragile (lowest HP with Wizard)
- Music buffs entire party (3+ round duration)
- Social interaction advantage (Charming)

**Optimal Play:**
- Position defensively
- Sing Bardic Music (continuous buff)
- Use Charming on NPC interactions
- Avoid direct combat if possible

**Tactical Tips:**
- Choose song based on encounter (offense/defense/healing)
- Stay back from melee
- Use Loremaster on puzzles/research

---

## CLASS 5: RANGER

**HP:** 10 | **CS:** d6-1

**Abilities:**
- Survivalist (wilderness reroll)
- Alert (initiative advantage + surprise resistance)
- Ranged Mastery (flexible ranged attacks)

**Role:** Ranged + Scout

**Mechanics:**
- Same HP as Fighter (tank-adjacent)
- Ranged Mastery enables any ranged weapon
- Alert gives initiative advantage
- Survivalist for exploration

**Optimal Play:**
- Ranged attacks at distance
- Use Alert to act early
- Survivalist for exploration/tracking
- Avoid direct melee

**Tactical Tips:**
- Stay at distance from melee
- Act early (initiative advantage)
- Scout with Survivalist

---

## CLASS 6: WIZARD

**HP:** 4 | **CS:** d6-2

**Abilities:**
- Brains (Intelligence reroll)
- Combat Magic (2 spells per encounter)
- Free Form Magic (2 spells per session, creative)

**Role:** Versatile + Control

**Mechanics:**
- Lowest HP (most fragile)
- Lowest CS (poor in combat)
- 2 spell options per encounter (offensive/control/support)
- Out-of-combat problem-solving (Free Form Magic)

**Optimal Play:**
- Cast spells (primary action economy)
- Avoid direct combat
- Position far from enemies
- Use Free Form Magic for exploration

**Tactical Tips:**
- Select spells for encounter type
- Use Confusion for crowd control
- Use Dark Bolt for reliable damage
- Stay out of melee range
