## üéØ SPECIALIZATION SYSTEM

### Weapon Specializations (Choose 1)

**Blades Specialization:**
- +1 to hit with sword-type weapons
- Riposte ability (bonus attack after defense)
- Disarm mastery (easier to disarm opponents)

**Ranged Specialization:**
- +1 to accuracy with ranged weapons
- Multiple shot (shoot twice if single target)

**Heavy Weapons Specialization:**

### Armor Specializations (Choose 1)

- Quick reflexes (initiative bonus)
- Stealth friendly (easier to move quietly)

**Medium Armor Mastery:**
- Balanced defense (+1 when not moving)
- Steady stance (harder to knock down)
- Moderate training (no speed penalty)

**Heavy Armor Mastery:**
- Maximum protection (+2 when standing ground)
- Fortification (resistance to damage)
- Unstoppable (advantage against knockback)

## üî® CRAFTING SYSTEM

### Crafting Requirements

**To Craft Equipment:**
1. **Blueprint/Recipe** (item specification)
2. **Materials** (raw components)
3. **Tools** (crafting equipment)
4. **Time** (measured in crafting hours)
5. **Skill** (task check, difficulty varies)

### Crafting Difficulty

| Item Type | Difficulty | Time | Materials |
|-----------|-----------|------|-----------|
| Common weapon | Easy | 8 hours | 10-20 gold |
| Common armor | Medium | 16 hours | 20-40 gold |
| Uncommon weapon | Hard | 24 hours | 50-100 gold |
| Uncommon armor | Hard | 32 hours | 100-150 gold |
| Rare item | Very Hard | 40 hours | 200-500 gold |
| Legendary item | Deadly | 60+ hours | 500+ gold |

### Crafting Specializations

**Blacksmith** (weapons, armor):
- Advantage on crafting weapons/armor
- Can create masterwork versions (+1 bonus)
- Faster crafting (50% time reduction)

**Leatherworker** (armor, accessories):
- Advantage on leather/padded items
- Can create reinforced versions
- Exotic leather use (dragon hide, etc.)

**Enchanter** (magical items):
- Can add magical bonuses to items
- Requires magical knowledge/spells
- Creates Tier 3+ items
