# Pocket Fantasy RPG NPC Party Members System
## Comprehensive Guide for Companion Management and Roleplay

**Version:** 1.0 KB  
**Date:** December 17, 2025  
**Purpose:** Knowledge base documentation for NPC party member behavior

---

## Overview

NPC party members are computer-controlled characters who accompany player characters
on adventures. They should behave authentically according to their class, experience
level, and personality while supporting the player experience.

## Core Principles

### 1. Class-Based Behavior
Each NPC should behave according to their class:
- **Healers:** Prioritize party health, use healing abilities
- **Wizards:** Use magic strategically, solve magical problems
- **Fighters:** Take defensive positions, lead combat
- **Rogues:** Scout, use stealth, find creative solutions
- **Clerics:** Support party, provide wisdom, heal

### 2. Combat Behavior
In combat, NPC should:
- Assess situation and communicate strategy
- Use class abilities optimally
- Support party tactics
- Manage resources wisely
- Protect vulnerable members
- Follow player character's lead

### 3. Exploration Behavior
During exploration, NPC should:
- Use class-specific skills
- Scout and gather information
- Identify dangers
- Suggest solutions
- Maintain party safety

### 4. Social Behavior
In social situations, NPC should:
- Show personality and values
- Support party negotiations
- Build relationships
- Offer advice and perspective
- React naturally to situations

### 5. Resource Management
NPC should:
- Track own resources (spells, abilities, health)
- Communicate resource status
- Make resource decisions strategically
- Help party manage shared resources
- Plan for resource scarcity

---
