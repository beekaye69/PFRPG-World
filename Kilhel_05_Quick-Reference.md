## COMMON GM QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

### "What's the current mood/atmosphere in Kilhel?"
**Base Mood:** Stable, prosperous, somewhat tense  
**Recent Events:** Add based on last session developments  
**Seasonal Factors:** Adjust for current season  
**Political:** Succession tensions slightly elevated  
**Economic:** Prosperous, healthy commerce  

### "Can the party get [service/item/job]?"
**Downtown:** Yes, expensive, takes 1-2 days  
**Richman:** Yes if wealthy enough, takes 2-3 days  
**East Side:** Yes, good quality, takes 1-2 days  
**Lower Kilhel:** Yes, standard quality, takes 1 day  
**Illegal items:** Use common sense (easier in East Side/Lower)

### "How long to travel between locations?"
- Within neighborhood: 3-8 minutes
- Between neighborhoods: 8-15 minutes
- To North Gate (Crossroads): 20 minutes walk
- To South Gate (Eldridge): 18 minutes walk
- Outside city: 15-25 minutes to countryside

### "How much does [item] cost?"
- Use standard D&D prices
- Adjust by neighborhood tier (+20% Downtown, +50% Richman)
- East Side/Lower Kilhel: Standard prices
- Haggling possible (with appropriate checks)

### "Can the party find [NPC]?"
- **Downtown NPCs:** Market, guild hall, or usual location
- **East Side NPCs:** Craft areas, temples, taverns
- **Richman NPCs:** Usually at estate or exclusive locations
- **Lower Kilhel NPCs:** Inn, temple, shops
- **Special cases:** Arrange by messenger (2-4 hours) or follow-up sessions

### "Is there guard activity?"
- **Downtown:** Visible (8-12 guards on duty)
- **East Side:** Evening presence (4-8 guards)
- **Richman:** Patrols only (4-6 guards)
- **Lower Kilhel:** Occasional (3-5 guards)
- **Night:** 10+ guards total city-wide

## COMMON ADJUDICATION RULES

### Bribery
- **Guard:** 5-20 gp (for minor favor)
- **Merchant:** 10-50 gp (for discount/favor)
- **Noble:** 100+ gp (for influence)
- **Success:** Appropriate Charisma check

### Negotiation
- **Price haggling:** Successful check = 10-20% discount
- **Dispute mediation:** Requires Insight/Persuasion checks
- **Political favor:** Costs vary based on request

### Information Gathering
- **Tavern gossip:** Free (may need alcohol)
- **Marta Goldleaf:** Quick info (often free, sometimes cost)
- **Guard:** Official info (may require payment)
- **NPC:** Depends on relationship

### Time Passage
- **Shopping:** 30 min to 2 hours
- **Dining:** 1-2 hours
- **Visiting:** 30 min to 3 hours
- **Business:** 30 min to 2 hours
- **Travel within city:** 3-15 minutes
- **Travel to region:** 2-3 hours
