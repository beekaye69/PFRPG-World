### Settlement Overview

**Population:** ~200 permanent residents  
**Governance:** Mayor Aldwin (aging merchant, pragmatic)  
**Economy:** Grain trade (primary), leather goods, general commerce, caravan hub  
**Defense:** Light palisade, Town Watch (8 guards), militia liaison to Crossroads  
**Atmosphere:** Bustling medieval market town, merchant-friendly, trade-focused, welcoming to adventurers  

**Notable Districts:**
- Market Square (north) - caravan hub, merchant stalls, town center
- Tavern Row (central) - social hub, lodging, information
- Forge District (south) - blacksmith, metalwork, crafting
- Residential (east/west) - housing, personal dwellings
- Grain Warehouses (south edge) - family property, storage, economy foundation

### MAYOR ALDWIN (Town Leader)

**Type:** Aging merchant, pragmatic politician  
**Age/Appearance:** 58, weathered face, merchant clothes, walking cane (affectation)  
**Location:** Market Square office / Town hall (small building, north side)  

**Background:**
Built Eldridge into a trade hub 25 years ago. Made first fortune on grain contracts. Politically shrewd, conflict-averse, profit-motivated but not ruthless. Widower (wife died 10 years past). Has two adult children in other cities.

**Personality:**
- Diplomatic but calculating
- Values order and commerce above all
- Suspicious of violence disrupting trade
- Appreciative of Gort's goblin defense (protects trade routes)
- Interested in adventurers only if they're profitable/controllable

**Motivation:**
- Keep Eldridge prosperous and safe
- Maintain good relationships with militia/Crossroads
- Expand trade (exploring new routes)
- Secure legacy for children (business stability)

**Ties to Gort:**
- Grateful for goblin defense (praised at town council)
- Offered 10gp bounty initially (paid after raid)
- Potential employer for escort quests (merchant protection)
- Neutral but friendly; will provide information freely

**Dialogue Examples:**
- "Gort, you've proven yourself invaluable. Eldridge owes you a debt. If there's a way I can repay it..."
- "Those goblins were hurting our bottom line. With them contained, merchants feel safe again."
- "Thorne at Crossroads speaks highly of you. Perhaps you'd consider permanent militia contract?"

**Services:**
- Town information (gossip, politics, merchant activity)
- Small loans (up to 50gp, 10% interest, 30-day terms)
- Caravan coordination (can arrange escorts through Karel)
- Town watch assignments (small jobs, 2-5gp)

**Reputation Interactions:**
- Gort's legendary status: Aldwin leverages it for trade credibility
- Merchants trust Eldridge more after goblin defeat
- New caravans arriving specifically to hire Gort as escort

### JORIK (Wagon Driver & Axeman)

**Type:** Sturdy laborer, adventure-curious, loyal companion, brotherly bond

**Motivation:**

- Build lasting friendship (brotherly bond)
- Gain combat experience (training)

**Ties to Gort:**
- **Permanent companion** (+1 morale when paired)

- Sees Gort as embodiment of courage he lacks
- Willing to follow into any danger
- Brother-figure bond (emotional depth)

**Dialogue Examples:**
- "Kael died thinking we were weak. I want to change that. I want to *be* strong like you, Gort."
- "Wherever you go next, I'm coming. That's not a request‚Äîthat's a vow."

**Combat Stats (If Used in Combat):**

- Special: Morale boost to Gort (+1 to all checks when paired)

**Skills/Abilities:**
- Excellent tracker (natural ability from wagon routes)

**Companion Mechanics:**

- Equipment: Can carry significant supplies (strong)

**Character Development:**
