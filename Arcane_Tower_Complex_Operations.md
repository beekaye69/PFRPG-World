- **Closed:** Sunday (Caldris's contemplation day)
- **After-hours:** By special appointment (emergency services available for premium fee)
- **Cancellation Policy:** 48 hours notice, or lose deposit

### COMMISSION PROCESS

1. **Consultation** (30 min, free)
   - Customer describes needed item
   - Caldris assesses feasibility
   - Elara quotes price and timeline

2. **Contract Agreement** (written)
   - Item specifications detailed
   - Price finalized (often negotiated)
   - 50% deposit required to start

3. **Production** (1-14 days depending on complexity)
   - Meric leads crafting
   - Lyssa supports with research/testing
   - Updates provided weekly

4. **Final Testing** (1-2 days)
