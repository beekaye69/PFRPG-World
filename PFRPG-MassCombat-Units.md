## SECTION 1: UNIT SCALE
In PFRPG, a **Unit** consists of roughly 100 soldiers (a "Block").
- **Unit HP:** 10 per Block.
- **Unit CS:** Determined by Troop Quality.
- **Unit Movement:** Standard 30ft per round (modified by terrain).

## SECTION 2: TROOP QUALITY TIERS
Troop quality replaces the "Level" and "Training" bonuses from the source PDF.

| Tier | Quality Name | CS Die | Base HP | Training Metadata |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0** | [Untrained] | d6-2 | 5 | Peasants, Levies, Rabble |
| **1** | [Militia] | d6-1 | 8 | Town Watch, Conscripts |
| **2** | [Soldier] | d6 | 10 | Professional Infantry, Mercenaries |
| **3** | [Veteran] | d6+1 | 12 | Elite Guards, Knights, Veterans |

## SECTION 3: UNIT SPECIALIZATIONS [TAGS]
Units carry specific mechanical tags that interact with field modifiers:
- `[Archers]`: Can strike from Range (3 Units away).
- `[Cavalry]`: +1 CS when Charging in open terrain.
- `[Pike]`: Negates `[Cavalry]` charge bonus.
- `[Undead]`: Immune to Morale/Fear Task Rolls.
