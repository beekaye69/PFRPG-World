# KILHEL CITY - COMPLETE SETTLEMENT SYSTEM
## Tier 1: Geography, Layout & Neighborhood Breakdown

**City Status:** Major city (2,000-2,500 residents)  
**Government:** Feudal (Duke's authority with noble advisors)  
**Neighborhoods:** 4 distinct districts (Downtown, East Side, Richman, Lower Kilhel)  
**Total Population:** 840 (per map) in organized districts  
**Area Coverage:** ~1.2 square miles (divided by neighborhood)  
**Campaign Scale:** Major hub, long-term base, political intrigue  

---

## 1. KILHEL CITY OVERVIEW

### City Profile
**Name:** Kilhel  
**Ruler:** Duke Aldric Valorain (age 58, human noble)  
**Type:** Feudal city-state with merchant council influence  
**Primary Functions:** Regional government, military center, trade hub, cultural capital  
**Architecture Style:** Ornate wooden buildings mixed with stone fortifications  
**Defining Features:** Visible class divisions, ducal castle, multiple temples, market diversity  

### Strategic Position
- Located in central region (midway between settlements)
- Access to major trade routes (north/south/east/west)
- Natural defensibility (slight elevation, surrounding estates)
- Water access (river for trade and power)
- Agricultural hinterland (sustains population)

### Racial Composition
**Overall City:**
- Human: 70-75% (dominant across all neighborhoods)
- Dwarf: 10-15% (concentrated in East Side)
- Elf: 5-8% (concentrated in Richman)
- Halfling: 2-3% (scattered, some in Lower Kilhel)
- Other: <1% (occasional visitors, adventurers)

## 5. CITY INFRASTRUCTURE & SYSTEMS

### Water Systems
- **Downtown:** Central fountain (Market Plaza), public access
- **East Side:** Community well, one per street block
- **Richman:** Private wells on estates, decorative fountains
- **Lower Kilhel:** Community wells (shared), reliable access

### Sanitation
- **East Side:** Most organized (dwarf influence), regular maintenance
- **Lower Kilhel:** Well-maintained, community standards
- **Downtown:** Regular cleaning (merchant standard)
- **Richman:** Private waste management (per estate)

### Roads & Transportation
- **Main roads:** Packed earth, cleared daily (merchant traffic)
- **Secondary roads:** Packed earth, maintained weekly
- **Neighborhood streets:** Narrower, less maintained
- **Carts:** Common for trade, restricted in some areas
- **Animals:** Stables in East Side, Richman, Lower Kilhel

### City Guard Presence
- **Downtown:** 8-12 guards (high visibility)
- **Richman:** 4-6 guards (patrol only)
- **East Side:** 4-8 guards (evening presence)
- **Lower Kilhel:** 3-5 guards (occasional patrol)
- **Night watch:** 10+ guards total (city-wide patrols)

### Public Services
- **Markets:** 3 locations (Downtown, East Side, Lower Kilhel)
- **Temples:** 4 locations (one per neighborhood)
- **Inns:** 3 major (Downtown, Lower Kilhel, one private Richman)
- **Guards:** Central garrison (near Downtown)
- **Officials:** Administrative offices (Downtown)

## 6. SEASONAL VARIATIONS & ATMOSPHERE

### Spring (Awakening)
- Markets expand (new goods)
- Construction activity increases
- Population increases (travelers arriving)
- Festivals begin (renewal celebrations)
- Prices increase slightly (+5%)

### Summer (Growth)
- Markets overflow (peak trading season)
- Agricultural goods abundant
- Temperature comfortable (evening outdoor activities)
- Entertainment/festivals frequent
- Tourist season (high visitor traffic)

### Autumn (Harvest)
- Markets focused on preservation goods
- Agricultural arrival (harvest season)
- Preparation for winter (supply stocking)
- Festivals (harvest celebrations)
- Prices peak (final season goods)

### Winter (Survival)
- Markets reduce (limited goods)
- Temperatures cold (activity moves indoors)
- Entertainment moves to inns/buildings
- Prices high (preserved goods scarcity)
- Population stable (minimal travel)
