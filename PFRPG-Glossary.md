## GLOSSARY ENTRIES

### A

#### Abilities (Class Abilities)
**Definition:** Unique mechanical powers granted to characters based on their chosen class. Each class receives specific abilities that differentiate playstyles and provide tactical advantages in and out of combat.

**Context:** Core to class differentiation in PFRPG. Classes are primarily distinguished by HP, CS, weapons/armor, and abilities.

**Mechanical Impact:** Class abilities often provide re-rolls on specific tasks, combat actions, or special effects. Enable diverse character archetypes and strategies.

**Availability:** Each class has 2-4 specific abilities. Some are always active (passive), while others are combat or task-specific.

**Examples:**
- [Righteous Insight](#righteous-insight-cleric-ability) (Cleric)
- [Muscle](#muscle-fighter-ability) (Fighter)
- [Nimble](#nimble-thief-ability) (Thief)
- [Combat Master](#combat-master-fighter-ability) (Fighter)

**See Also:** [Class](#class), [Fighter](#fighter), [Cleric](#cleric), [Thief](#thief), [Bard](#bard), [Ranger](#ranger), [Wizard](#wizard)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#character-classes

#### Acolyte
**Definition:** Generic humanoid monster type, typically representing religious servants or monks. Often found in temples or ruins with religious significance.

**Context:** Appears as potential encounter type when rolling humanoid monsters in dungeon generation.

**Mechanical Impact:** Generally uses d6-2 CS (average skilled), 4-6 hp depending on PM determination.

**See Also:** [Monster](#monster), [Humanoid](#humanoid), [Combat Skill](#combat-skill)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#monster-creation--abilities

#### Agility (Core Ability)
**Definition:** One of five core abilities determining character aptitude in dexterity, balance, and speed. Governs tasks requiring quick reflexes and physical coordination.

**Context:** Primary ability score affecting Thief and Ranger effectiveness, defensive positioning, and dodge-based tasks.

**Task Applications:**
- Balancing on ledges
- Dodging tumbling boulders
- Moving silently
- Hiding in shadows
- Climbing
- Disarming traps (Thief specialty)
- Unlocking mechanisms (Thief specialty)

**Class Bonuses:** 
- [Thief](#thief) gains re-roll on all agility tasks via [Nimble](#nimble-thief-ability)
- [Ranger](#ranger) benefits indirectly through [Ranged Mastery](#ranged-mastery-ranger-ability)

**See Also:** [Strength](#strength-core-ability), [Intelligence](#intelligence-core-ability), [Wisdom](#wisdom-core-ability), [Charisma](#charisma-core-ability), [Task Roll](#task-roll)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#core-abilities-mapped-to-tasks

#### Amulet of False Life
**Definition:** Magical jewelry granting temporary additional hit points for single encounter. Stacks above maximum hp ceiling.

**Mechanical Impact:** 
- Grant 6 temporary hp for current encounter only
- Can exceed character's maximum hp
- Takes combat action to activate
- Once per session usage

**Item Type:** [Magical Item](#magical-item) (jewelry/amulet)

**Restrictions:** Only 1 amulet/necklace can provide benefits simultaneously (per jewelry limit rule)

**Strategic Use:** Emergency survivability tool for fragile classes like [Wizard](#wizard)

**See Also:** [Amulet of Legends](#amulet-of-legends), [Magical Item](#magical-item), [Combat Action](#combat-action)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#amulet-of-false-life

#### Amulet of Legends
**Definition:** Magical jewelry that bonds to wearer and grants renewable Heroic Rerolls. Self-renews at session start.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Bestow 2 [Heroic Reroll](#heroic-reroll)s on first wearing
- Resets to 2 rerolls at start of each session
- Item bonds to wearer (exclusive use)
- Only works for bonded character

**Item Type:** [Magical Item](#magical-item) (jewelry/amulet)

**Strategic Value:** Best amulet for sustained heroic reroll resource generation

**See Also:** [Amulet of the Sun God](#amulet-of-the-sun-god), [Heroic Reroll](#heroic-reroll), [Magical Item](#magical-item)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#amulet-of-legends

#### Amulet of the Sun God
**Definition:** Magical jewelry that bonds to wearer and provides automatic full healing after each combat encounter.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Fully heal all hp after combat encounters

- Item bonds to wearer (exclusive use)
- Only works for bonded character

**Item Type:** [Magical Item](#magical-item) (jewelry/amulet)

**Strategic Value:** Excellent for tanks and melee-focused characters. Reduces healing potion reliance.

**See Also:** [Amulet of Legends](#amulet-of-legends), [Healing](#healing-rest--death), [Magical Item](#magical-item)

#### Amulet of the Void
**Definition:** Magical jewelry enabling movement through physical obstacles and objects. Single-use per activation with positioning benefits.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Takes combat action to activate
- Next round can pass through obstacle/object
- If passing through monster: neither creature may attack each other during activated round
- Useful for tactical repositioning

**Item Type:** [Magical Item](#magical-item) (jewelry/amulet)

**Strategic Use:** Escape positioning, bypass traps, flank maneuvers

**See Also:** [Combat Action](#combat-action), [Magical Item](#magical-item), [Movement](#movement-in-combat)

**Context:** Used in monster descriptions and [Monster Ability](#monster-ability) definitions.

**Mechanical Impact:** Often grants special immunities or vulnerability traits specific to animation type.

#### Arcane Armor (Spell)
**Definition:** Combat magic spell providing defensive enhancement. Target gains universal CS d6 on all defense actions.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Target makes all [Defense Action](#defense-action)s at CS d6 (regardless of class CS penalty)
- Re-roll all defense rolls, take best result
- Duration: Entire encounter
- Works with all classes equally

**Casting:** Combat spell, takes combat action, counts toward 2 spells per encounter for [Wizard](#wizard)

**Strategic Use:** Makes low-CS classes competitive defensively. Single roll can transform character survivability.

**Spell Type:** Combat magic

**See Also:** [Dark Bolt](#dark-bolt-spell), [Rain of Fire](#rain-of-fire-spell), [Confusion](#confusion-spell), [Magic Spell List](#magic-spell-list), [Wizard](#wizard)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#arcane-armor-spell

#### Arcane Gem
**Definition:** Magical crafting component used to imbue mundane weapons and armor with magical properties. Three gems create magical item.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- 3 gems + weapon/armor + blacksmith = [Focus](#focus-magical-weapons) x1 (weapon) or [Deflect](#deflect-magical-armor) x1 (armor)
- Gems consumed in crafting process
- Created through monster loot or found in treasure

**Item Value:** 250 gp per gem

**Loot Acquisition:** 
- Boss encounters: each character rolls d6, result 3-4 = 1 gem found
- Moderate/difficult encounters: rare

**Crafting Requirements:**
- Arcane Gems destroyed in process
- Requires blacksmith NPC
- Creates permanent magical enhancement

**See Also:** [Focus](#focus-magical-weapons), [Deflect](#deflect-magical-armor), [Magical Item](#magical-item), [Loot](#loot-distribution), [Runic Forge](#runic-forge)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#magical-weapons--armor

#### Armored (Monster Ability)
**Definition:** Monster ability granting damage reduction. Monster takes 1 less damage after successful [Defense Action](#defense-action) blocks damage.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- After defense roll reduces damage
- Additional -1 applied to final damage
- Cumulative with other damage reductions
- Effective defensive multiplier

**Counter:** [Steelbender](#steelbender-weapon) weapon negates this ability

**Tactics:** Requires higher CS rolls to overcome additional reduction layer

**See Also:** [Defense Action](#defense-action), [Monster Ability](#monster-ability), [Combat Skill](#combat-skill), [Steelbender](#steelbender-weapon)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#armored-monster-ability

#### Attack Action
**Definition:** Combat action where character attempts to deal damage to enemy. Standard offensive action in combat.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Takes 1 combat action per round
- Roll [Combat Skill](#combat-skill) die
- Result = damage dealt
- Target rolls defense action
- Damage reduced by defense value

**Attack Types:**
- [Melee Attack Action](#melee-attack-action)
- [Ranged Attack Action](#ranged-attack-action)

**Resolution:** Attacker rolls, then defender rolls, damage calculated

**See Also:** [Combat Action](#combat-action), [Melee Attack Action](#melee-attack-action), [Ranged Attack Action](#ranged-attack-action), [Defense Action](#defense-action), [Combat Skill](#combat-skill)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#attack-actions

### B

#### Backstab (Thief Ability)
**Definition:** Specialized [Thief](#thief) ability allowing devastating sneak attack on unaware targets. Doubles attack rolls for combined damage.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Target must be completely unaware and not in combat
- Success on [Agility](#agility-core-ability) task triggers ability
- Make 2 CS d6 [Melee Attack Action](#melee-attack-action)s
- Combine both damages into single attack roll
- May automatically surprise target (PM discretion)
- [Heroic Reroll](#heroic-reroll) expended re-rolls both attacks

**Tactical Use:** Ambush opener before combat starts. Prevents enemy first-round action via surprise mechanics.

**Limitations:**
- Only works on unaware targets
- Only works outside active combat
- Requires agility task success

**See Also:** [Thief](#thief), [Nimble](#nimble-thief-ability), [Flank](#flank-thief-ability), [Melee Attack Action](#melee-attack-action), [Agility](#agility-core-ability)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#backstab-thief-ability

#### Bag of Many Things
**Definition:** Magical equipment item enabling item conjuration. Access to free item creation once per session (combat/out-of-combat).

**Mechanical Impact:**
- In combat: 1 conjuring use
- Out of combat: 2 conjuring uses per session
- Produce any physical item desired (PM discretion)
- Carries items larger than bag exterior (infinite storage, PM discretion)
- Problem-solving tool for creative players

**Item Type:** [Magical Item](#magical-item) (equipment)

**Strategic Value:** Bypasses item acquisition limitations. Enables creative solutions.

**See Also:** [Magical Item](#magical-item), [Free Form Magic](#free-form-magic), [Combat Action](#combat-action)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#bag-of-many-things

#### Ballad of Legends (Bard Song)
**Definition:** [Bardic Music](#bardic-music-special-ability) spell providing enhanced [Heroic Reroll](#heroic-reroll) mechanics. Lowers heroic reroll triggering threshold.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Duration: Continuous while singing
- Effect: All party members gain [Heroic Reroll](#heroic-reroll) chance when rolling 3+ (instead of natural 6)
- Significantly increases reroll frequency
- Applies to all d6 rolls during song duration

**Targets:** All allies in hearing range

**Singing Duration:** Minimum 3 combat rounds before changing songs

**Strategic Value:** Magnifies reroll resource generation significantly. Best for luck-dependent builds.

**See Also:** [Bard](#bard), [Bardic Music](#bardic-music-special-ability), [Heroic Reroll](#heroic-reroll), [Song of Mending](#song-of-mending-bard-song), [Hymn of the Mighty](#hymn-of-the-mighty-bard-song)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#ballad-of-legends-bard-song

#### Band of Demons
**Definition:** Magical ring providing immunity to elemental and magical effects. Protects against fire, dark magic, and specific spells.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Immunity to [Dark Bolt](#dark-bolt-spell) spell
- Immunity to [Confusion](#confusion-spell) spell
- Immunity to [Rain of Fire](#rain-of-fire-spell) spell
- Immunity to all fire forms
- Broad elemental protection advantage

**Item Type:** [Magical Item](#magical-item) (jewelry/ring)

**Restrictions:** Only 2 rings can provide benefits simultaneously

**Counter:** Monsters with fire attacks or dark magic rendered ineffective

**See Also:** [Ring of Night](#ring-of-night), [Nether Band](#nether-band), [Ring of Protection](#ring-of-protection), [Magical Item](#magical-item)

#### Bard
**Definition:** Character class specializing in magic music, charisma, and versatile combat. Lowest CS class but gains party-wide buffs through song magic.

**Class Statistics:**
- Allowed races: All
- Hit points: 6 hp
- Combat Skill: d6-2 (lowest of all classes)
- Weapons: Any 1-handed weapon, bow
- Armor: Leather or padded

**Class Abilities:**
- [Charming](#charming-bard-ability): Re-roll charisma tasks
- [Loremaster](#loremaster-bard-ability): Re-roll knowledge tasks
- [Bardic Music](#bardic-music-special-ability): Cast magical songs for party buffs

**Bardic Songs:**
- [Hymn of the Mighty](#hymn-of-the-mighty-bard-song)
- [Lay of Deflection](#lay-of-deflection-bard-song)
- [Ballad of Legends](#ballad-of-legends-bard-song)
- [Song of Mending](#song-of-mending-bard-song)
- [Chant of Renewal](#chant-of-renewal-bard-song)
- [Melody of Madness](#melody-of-madness-bard-song)
- [Shanty of Pain](#shanty-of-pain-bard-song)

**Playstyle:** Support character providing party-wide buffs, low personal damage but significant force multiplier

**Tactical Role:** Party leader, morale booster, crowd control via song effects

**See Also:** [Charming](#charming-bard-ability), [Loremaster](#loremaster-bard-ability), [Bardic Music](#bardic-music-special-ability), [Class](#class), [Charisma](#charisma-core-ability)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#bard

#### Bardic Music (Special Ability)
**Definition:** [Bard](#bard) class signature ability enabling magical song casting. Songs provide party-wide effects during combat or exploration.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Minimum 3 combat rounds of singing before changing songs
- Can perform other actions while singing (including combat)
- All song targets must be within hearing range
- Takes combat action to begin singing
- No action cost to maintain after initiation

**Song List:**

**Strategic Value:** Continuous party buffs without action cost after initialization. Enables multi-action turns.

#### Bind Wounds (Cleric Ability)
**Definition:** [Cleric](#cleric) class ability enhancing post-encounter [Healing](#healing-rest--death). Provides increased automatic healing.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Post-encounter ability (no combat use)
- Heal 2 hp to all party characters after encounters
- Can heal wounded NPCs

- Passive application after combat (no action required)

**Usage:** Automatically applies whenever healing is appropriate after encounter

**Strategic Value:** Increases party sustain, reduces healing potion reliance

#### Black Lotus Poison
**Definition:** Magical poison applied to weapons enabling guaranteed [Poison](#poison-monster-ability) infliction on targets.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Paint on weapon or quiver of arrows
- When weapon deals ≥1 damage: Target afflicted with poison [Monster Ability](#monster-ability)
- Target automatically fails [Tough](#tough-task-difficulty) strength save
- Application takes 1 combat action to apply
- Duration: Entire combat encounter
- Some monsters may be immune (PM discretion)

**Item Type:** [Magical Item](#magical-item) (consumable)

**Strategic Use:** Guarantees poison effect without task roll. Useful against tough enemies.

**See Also:** [Poison](#poison-monster-ability), [Magical Item](#magical-item), [Weapon](#weapon), [Combat Action](#combat-action)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#black-lotus-poison

#### Blinkstrike (Magical Weapon)
**Definition:** Magical spear combining ranged attacks with initiative advantage. Returning property enables repeated ranged use.

**Mechanical Impact:**

#### Blocked Damage
**Definition:** Damage negated by successful [Defense Action](#defense-action). Subtracted from incoming damage total.

**Mechanical Impact:**
- Defender rolls [Combat Skill](#combat-skill) die
- Result = blocked damage amount
- Subtracted from attack damage
- Remaining damage reduces target hp

**Formula:** Incoming Damage - Blocked Damage = Net Damage

**Example:** 5 damage attack, 2 blocked = 3 net damage

**See Also:** [Defense Action](#defense-action), [Combat Skill](#combat-skill), [Damage](#damage), [Combat](#combat)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#defense-actions

#### Boss (Monster Classification)
**Definition:** Special encounter-ending monster. Typically end-of-level threats requiring coordinated party action.

**Dungeon Generation:** One Boss per dungeon level depth

**Boss Placement:** Decided near end of allotted playtime or when advancing deeper

**Boss Selection:** PM chooses based on party combat efficiency

**Boss Scaling:** Can use normal monster stats (CS d6, double hp or more) or powerful creature defaults

**Loot:** Boss encounters generate enhanced loot (d6x100 gp, magical item rolls)

**Strategic Differences:** Boss immunities, enhanced abilities, higher hp pools

**See Also:** [Monster](#monster), [Encounter](#encounter), [Loot](#loot-distribution), [Dungeon Generator](#dungeon-generator)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#bosses

#### Boss Challenge
**Definition:** Highest difficulty level for room/encounter assessment. Triggers Boss-level loot rewards.

**Loot Determination:** Party defeated Boss monster requiring significant resources

**Gold Reward:** d6x100 gp (re-roll, take best)

**Magical Item Rolls:** Each character rolls d6
- 1-2: Nothing additional
- 3-4: [Arcane Gem](#arcane-gem)
- 5: [Focus](#focus-magical-weapons) x1 weapon OR [Deflect](#deflect-magical-armor) x1 armor (player choice)

#### Defense Action
**Definition:** Reactive combat action reducing incoming damage. Always available, always rolled when targeted.

**Defense Resolution:**
1. Attacker rolls [Combat Skill](#combat-skill) die
2. Target rolls [Combat Skill](#combat-skill) die (determines blocked damage)
3. Blocked damage subtracted from attack result
4. Remaining damage reduces target hp

**Formula:** Incoming Damage - Blocked Damage = Net Damage

**Mandatory:** Cannot be refused; always rolled when targeted by attack

**Full Defense:** Can forego [Attack Action](#attack-action) for enhanced defense (re-roll all defenses)

**See Also:** [Combat](#combat), [Attack Action](#attack-action), [Combat Skill](#combat-skill), [Full Defense](#full-defense-action)

#### Gift of Kings (Magical Armor)
**Definition:** Magical armor suit enabling in-combat healing and increased post-encounter healing. Support armor.

- Post-Encounter Bonus: Heal 1 extra hp after each encounter
- Combined healing boost

**Item Type:** [Magical Item](#magical-item) (armor)

**Class Restriction:** Cleric, Fighter

**Strategic Value:** Increases party sustain significantly

**See Also:** [Magical Armor](#magical-armor), [Healing](#healing-rest--death), [Combat Action](#combat-action)

#### Gold Pieces (gp)
**Definition:** Currency in PFRPG world. Loot reward from encounters.

**Loot Examples:**
- Easy Challenge: Each character finds d6 gp
- Moderate Challenge: Each character finds d6 gp (re-roll, take best)
- Difficult Challenge: Each character finds d6x10 gp (re-roll, take best)
- Boss Challenge: Party finds d6x100 gp (re-roll, take best)

**Item Values:**
- Mundane items: d6 gp
- Adventuring equipment: d6x10 gp
- Unique items: 200 gp
- [Arcane Gem](#arcane-gem): 250 gp
- Magical weapons/armor: 750 gp

**See Also:** [Loot Distribution](#loot-distribution), [Treasure](#treasure)

**Cross-Reference:** PFRPG-Analysis.md#loot-distribution
