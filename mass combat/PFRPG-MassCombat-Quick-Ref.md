## The "Session Zero" Reference Page

---

## 1. COMBAT SEQUENCE (ROUND)
1. **Command:** Commanders roll Initiative (Agility Task).
2. **Volley:** `[Archers]` and Siege Engines fire.
3. **Maneuver:** Units move/reposition.
4. **The Clash:** Engaged units roll simultaneous CS.
5. **Morale:** Units with heavy losses check Wisdom (4+).

## 2. DAMAGE CALCULATION
> **(Attacker CS Roll) - (Defender CS Roll) = Unit HP Damage.**
> *Minimum damage 1 (unless Deflected).*

## 3. SIEGE THRESHOLDS
- **Climb Wall:** Agility Task (5+)
- **Ram Gate:** Strength Task (6)
- **Wizard Fireball:** 2d6 damage to unit or wall.

## 4. MORALE MODIFIERS (Wisdom 4+)
- **-1 Difficulty:** Unit is winning/holding high ground.
- **+1 Difficulty:** Unit lost >3 HP this round.
- **+2 Difficulty:** Commander was killed/fled.
