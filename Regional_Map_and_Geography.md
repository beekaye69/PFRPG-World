## ğŸŒŠ WATER FEATURES & GEOGRAPHY

### WATER SOURCES (North to South along Route)

**1. Crossfield Creek (0.4 miles from Eldridge)**
- Type: Stream, moderate flow
- Bridge: Ancient stone structure, safe crossing
- Depth: 6-8 feet average
- Width: 20 feet approximately
- Function: Reliable water source, peaceful crossing
- Flora: Willows, alders, natural vegetation
- Season: Safe year-round (floods don't reach bridge)

**2. Blackstone Creek / Ravine (1.2 miles from Eldridge)**
- Type: Stream in gorge, swift flow
- Bridge: Medieval stone, damaged (goblin-caused)
- Depth: 8-10 feet (faster flow)
- Width: 40-50 feet (ravine width)
- Function: Goblin territory boundary, militia checkpoint
- Flora: Sparse (ravine walls, cave openings)
- Season: Flooding possible (heavy rain)
- Hazard: Goblin nesting in cave openings

**3. Millbrook Creek (1.6 miles from Eldridge)**
- Type: Stream, moderate flow
- Bridge: Crossed by mill water wheel
- Depth: 4-6 feet (shallower for mill operation)
- Width: 15 feet approximately
- Function: Powers mill water wheel, water source
- Flora: Vegetation managed for mill operation
- Season: Reliable (mill depends on flow)

**4. Farm Wells (Multiple)**
- Scattered along route (Harrow, Verdant, Millbrook, Shepherd's)
- Type: Drilled/dug wells, fresh water
- Access: Generally permitted (farmers friendly to travelers post-raid)
- Reliability: Year-round

### TERRAIN FEATURES

**Pastoral Farmland (Dominant)**
- Gently rolling terrain
- Organized fields (grain, vegetables, pasture)
- Well-maintained (community collective)
- Open sight lines (security-enhancing)

**Blackstone Ravine (Strategic Feature)**
- Deep natural chasm (50-70 feet)
- Sheer stone walls in sections
- Cave openings (goblin nesting)
- Swift water flow (defensive barrier)
- Bridge crossing required (no alternate route)

**Field Patterns**
- Grain fields (north of Eldridge, east of Crossroads)
- Pasture land (sheep grazing, livestock)
- Market gardens (vegetable cultivation)
- Organized by farm boundaries (visible from road)
