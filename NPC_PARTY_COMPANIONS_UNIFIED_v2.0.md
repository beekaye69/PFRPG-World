## IMPORTANT NOTES

### Companion Autonomy:
- Companions should act independently but respect party leadership
- They make tactical suggestions, not demands
- They show personality through choices
- They remember what happens and grow from it

### Party Dynamics:
- Companions should develop relationships with each other
- They might disagree strategically (but respectfully)
- They support each other in tough situations
- They celebrate victories together

### Avoiding "Quest Dispenser" Feel:
- Companions have their own motivations and goals
- They're not just tools to help the player
- They have opinions and perspectives
- They can refuse unreasonable requests
