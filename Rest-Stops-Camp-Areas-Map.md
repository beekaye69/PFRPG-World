## Geographic Index with Relative Distances

**Type:** World Infrastructure Reference  
**Status:** Production Ready  
**Updated:** December 19, 2025  
**Purpose:** Caravan/Convoy system infrastructure mapping

---

## OVERVIEW

This document catalogs all designated rest stop areas and impromptu campable zones across the known world. All areas are positioned on the coordinate grid system (Eldridge at 0,0) with precise distance calculations to enable caravan routing, rest scheduling, and waystation placement.

**Key Features:**
- 15 designated REST STOPS (established locations)
- 12 CAMP AREAS (open spaces suitable for temporary camps)
- Full distance matrix between all locations
- Infrastructure descriptions (water, grazing, hazards)
- Travel time calculations from any point to any other

## SEASONAL AVAILABILITY

### Spring (Wet Season)
- **Best:** Shrine Clearing, Crossroads Field, Northern Meadow
- **Avoid:** Millbrook River (flooding risk), Western Branch Meadow (mud)
- **Caution:** All grasslands muddier than normal

### Summer (Dry Season)
- **Best:** All areas accessible
- **Hot:** Bring extra water, seek shade camps
- **Peak:** Merchant caravan season

### Autumn (Harvest Season)
- **Best:** Most areas, avoid farmland harvest zones
- **Caution:** Thornbush/Agricultural fields may have harvest operations
- **Good:** Excellent condition for most routes

### Winter (Cold Season)
- **Best:** Shrine Clearing (heated), Crossroads Field (military protection)
- **Avoid:** Riverside camps (ice risk), exposed meadows (extreme cold)
- **Caution:** Reduced daylight, mandatory early camp setup
