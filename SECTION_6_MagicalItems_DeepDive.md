## FOCUS WEAPONS (OFFENSE-BOOSTING)

### Categories of Focus Weapons

**General Purpose (Elemental/Bonus Damage):**
- Steelbender: Armor penetration
- Ember Death: Fire damage
- Tooth: Persistent bleed damage

**Specialized (Creature Type):**
- Blackout: +1 vs. Undead
- Scar of Giants: +1 vs. Large
- Vex of Giants: +2 vs. Large
- Brain Peeker: +1 vs. Humanoid

**Utility (Special Effects):**
- Edge of Night: Stealth bonus
- Bone Stick: Skeleton summoning
- Mender: Life steal (self-healing)
- Trinity: Triple attack
- Execution: Instant kill (humanoid)

**Wizard-Specific:**
- Celestial Branch: Flexible magic focus
- Staff of Replenishment: Healing per attack
- Crone's Limb: Spell damage bonus
- Staff of Guardian: Damage reduction

### Weapon Selection by Class

| Class | Best Focus | Why |
|-------|-----------|-----|
| Fighter | Any (synergizes all) | Combat Master explosions |
| Thief | Edge of Night | Positioning advantage |
| Ranger | Bow of Ages | Ranged snipe potential |
| Cleric | Clarity | Holy Light access |
| Bard | Darkheart | Dark Bolt access |
| Wizard | Celestial Branch | Flexible dual-slot |

## DEFLECT ARMOR (DEFENSE-BOOSTING)

### Categories of Deflect Armor

**Shield-Based:**
- Vigilant Shield: Ranged defense reroll
- Fate Shield: Immunities + retaliation
- Last Guard: Deflect recharge
- Vicious Buckler: Ranged + counter-attack

**Plate-Based:**
- Gift of Kings: Combat healing
- Defiance: Negate 3 attacks
- Oathkeeper: Proxy defense

**Specialty Items:**
- Helm of Protection: Ability immunity
- Bracers of Reflection: Ranged reflection
- Boots of Wall Walking: Positioning freedom
- Horn of Heroes: Party Heroic Reroll grant

### Armor Selection by Playstyle

| Playstyle | Best Deflect | Why |
|-----------|-----------|-----|
| Tank | Gift of Kings | Healing while defending |
| Support | Horn of Heroes | Party resource sharing |
| Specialized | By effect | Match encounter type |

## JEWELRY SYSTEM

### Rings (Max 2 Active)

**Types:**
- Ring of Night (dark vision, shadow magic)
- Ring of Protection (-1 damage taken)
- Ring of Senses (perception reroll)
- Ring of Speed (initiative advantage)
- Band of Demons (fire/dark immunity)
- Nether Band (energy drain immunity)
- Ring of Fire Worm (breath resistance)

### Necklaces (Max 1 Active)

**Types:**
- Amulet of Legends: +2 Heroic Rerolls/session (BEST)
- Amulet of Sun God: Full post-encounter healing
- Amulet of False Life: Temporary HP
- Amulet of Void: Phasing movement
- Necklace of Damned: Shadow summoning
- Plagued Bone Fetish: Retaliation damage
- Pendant of Titan: Strength tasks simplified

### Jewelry Priority

**First Priority:** Amulet of Legends (+2 Heroic Rerolls is huge)

**Second Priority:** Ring of Protection (-1 damage on all hits is reliable)

**Third Priority:** Situational to encounter (Ring of Speed for initiative, Amulet of Sun God for healing-focused)

## CRAFTING SYSTEM

### Crafting Requirements

**Base Recipe (Any Magical Item):**
- 3 Arcane Gems
- 1 Base weapon or armor
- Blacksmith NPC (PM approval)

**Upgrade Path (Runic Forge):**
- x1 → x2: 3 additional Arcane Gems (6 total)
- x2 → x3: 3 additional Arcane Gems (9 total)

### Gem Collection Strategy

**Early Campaign:** Collect 3 gems for first x1 item
**Mid Campaign:** Collect 6 gems for x2 item or second x1
**Late Campaign:** Collected 9+ gems for x3 items
