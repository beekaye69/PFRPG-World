## OVERVIEW

Four playable character races:
1. **Dwarf** - Underground specialist
2. **Elf** - Keen-eyed, magic-resistant
3. **Halfling** - Stealthy, excellent in hiding
4. **Human** - Flexible, resource advantage

---

## RACE 1: DWARF

**Traits:**
- Dark Vision (60ft in darkness)
- Underground Expertise (Average 4+ difficulty in dungeons)
- Poison Resistance (advantage vs. poison)

**Optimal Classes:** Cleric, Fighter

**Best For:** Dungeon crawling, defensive play

**Mechanics:**
- Dark vision enables underground exploration without light
- Underground Expertise helps with navigation/survival underground
- Poison resistance situational but valuable

---

## RACE 2: ELF

**Traits:**
- Dark Vision (60ft in darkness)
- Secret Door Finding (Simple 3+ difficulty finding secrets)
- Paralysis Immunity (can't be paralyzed)
- Surprise Resistance (only surprised on roll of 1)

**Optimal Classes:** Ranger, Wizard

**Best For:** Exploration, combat security

**Mechanics:**
- Best exploration race (secret doors + dark vision)
- Immune to paralysis (huge advantage vs. certain monsters)
- Surprise resistance gives massive ambush advantage

---

## RACE 3: HALFLING

**Traits:**
- Large Monster Defense (reroll vs. large enemies)
- Forest Hiding (laughable 2+ difficulty hiding in forests)
- Hidden State (undetectable when hidden)

**Optimal Classes:** Thief

**Best For:** Stealth, avoiding large threats

**Mechanics:**
- Forest Hiding enables easy concealment in wilderness
- Hidden State combined with Thief Backstab = powerful combo
- Large Monster Defense helps against boss monsters

---

## RACE 4: HUMAN

**Traits:**
- +1 Heroic Reroll per session (total: 2 instead of 1)

**Optimal Classes:** Any (flexible)

**Best For:** Any playstyle, resource advantage

**Mechanics:**
- Extra Heroic Reroll = 100% more resource
- Most flexible race (works with any class)
- Simple but powerful benefit
