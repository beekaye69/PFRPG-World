# PFRPG MASS COMBAT: UNIT GENERATOR
## System: v4.2 | Logic: Quality-to-CS Mapping

---

## SECTION 1: THE CORE UNIT [BLOCK]
A standard **Unit** (Block) consists of 100 individuals. 
- **Base HP:** 10
- **Base CS:** d6-2 (Untrained)

## SECTION 2: QUALITY UPGRADES
Spend "Quality Points" (QP) or assign based on unit background.

| Tier | Quality | CS Die | QP Cost | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0** | Untrained | d6-2 | 0 | Peasants, Levies |
| **1** | Militia | d6-1 | 1 | Town Watch, Conscripts |
| **2** | Soldier | d6 | 3 | Professional Army |
| **3** | Veteran | d6+1 | 5 | Elite Guard, Knights |

## SECTION 3: UNIT SPECIALIZATION TAGS [MAPPING]
Assign up to 2 tags per unit. Tags modify behavior or grant [Heroic Rerolls].

| Tag | Mechanical Benefit | PFRPG Requirement |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| `[Archers]` | Can attack from 3 Units away. | Must have Bows/Crossbows |
| `[Cavalry]` | [Heroic Reroll] on Charge in Open Terrain. | Must have Mounts |
| `[Pike]` | Negates `[Cavalry]` Charge bonus. | Must have Reach weapons |
| `[Heavy]` | Defender rolls CS with [Heroic Reroll] vs Melee. | Must have Plate/Chain |
| `[Flying]` | Ignore Ground Terrain; Cannot be Melee Clashed. | Pegasus, Griffons, Magic |
