## üìñ CORE COMBAT MECHANICS REFERENCE

### Combat Structure (From PFRPG Rulebook)

**Combat Rounds:**
- Series of actions from 2+ sides engaged in battle
- Each side takes actions, then round ends
- Combat continues until one side is defeated

**Surprise:**
- Roll d6 on each side
- Rolling 1-2 = that side loses first round actions
- If unaware of other's presence

**Initiative:**
- Agility-based task
- Roll d6 for each side
- Higher/equal rolls go before lower rolls
- Players choose action order among themselves
- Initiative NOT re-rolled each round

**Movement & Attack:**
- Character may move THEN attack OR attack THEN move
- If moving away from engaged enemy: stationary enemy gets free normal Melee Attack

**Range:**
- Melee = adjacent enemy
- Ranged = longer distance (weapon/ability range per PM discretion)

**Combat Skill (CS):**
- Used for both Attack and Defence
- Melee and Ranged actions work same way

**Attack Action:**
- Roll CS die
- Result = damage done to enemy

**Defence Action:**
- Target rolls CS die
- Result = blocked damage
- Remaining damage = HP loss

**Full Defence:**
- Forego Attack Action at start of round
- Re-roll ALL Defence Actions that round, taking best result
- Effect lasts until start of next round

**Example Calculation:**
```
Attacker: CS d6, rolls 4 = 4 damage
Defender: CS d6-1, rolls 3, -1 = 2 blocked
Enemy takes: 4 - 2 = 2 damage
```

### FIGHTER PROFILE

**Core Stats:**
- HP: 10 (highest starting HP)
- CS: d6 (full die, no modifier)
- Melee & Ranged: Any weapon, any armor
- Role: Primary damage dealer, tank, leader

**Combat Strengths:**
1. **Consistent Damage:** CS d6 means solid attack rolls (average 3.5 damage per hit)
2. **Multiple Actions:** Can attack + move, or move + attack
3. **Challenge Ability:** Direct attacks to fighter, control enemy actions
4. **Combat Master:** Melee attacks explode on 6, get bonus d6 roll added
5. **High HP:** 10 HP (tied for highest)
6. **Armor Flexibility:** Any armor type

**Combat Weaknesses:**
1. **No Range Advantage:** No bonus to ranged attacks
2. **No Healing:** Can't self-heal in combat
3. **No Magic:** Can't cast spells or special abilities
4. **No Spell Slots:** Purely physical-based combat
5. **No Crowd Control:** Can't stop multiple enemies

**Optimal Combat Positioning:**
- Front line (adjacent to enemies)
- Between ranged allies and melee enemies
- Close enough to use Challenge ability
- NOT isolated (need support healing)

**Resource Management:**
- No spells or limited abilities
- Can use Focus/Deflect equipment
- Heroic Rerolls crucial for tough fights
- Challenge ability: 1 per encounter

**Fighter Combat Strategy:**

**Against Single Strong Enemy (Troll, Minotaur, Dragon):**
1. Use Challenge to lock down its attacks
2. Position allies behind for ranged/spell support
3. Use Focus on weapon when rolls are good
4. Defend allies when they're in danger
5. Apply exploding die combat rolls for massive damage
6. Rely on Combat Master to multiply damage through explosions

**Against Multiple Weak Enemies:**
1. Attack + Move to surround and control space
2. Take full Defence if overwhelmed
3. Focus on eliminating one at a time
4. Use challenge to prevent ambushes
5. Protect squishy allies (wizard, healer)

**Against Mixed Encounters:**
1. Use Challenge on strongest/most dangerous
2. Let ranged allies handle others
3. Switch targets when Challenge ends
4. Play tanking role (take damage for allies)
5. Use Challenge strategically on most threatening enemy

**Fighter Combat Example: Fighter vs Troll**
```
SETUP:
Fighter: HP 10/10, CS d6, Focus x1 sword
Troll: HP 18/18, CS d6, Strong ability (+1 extra damage)

ROUND 1 - Fighter Initiative (rolls 5, Troll rolls 3):

- Uses Challenge ability: "Troll, face me!"

- Troll Defence: rolls 2, takes 3 damage
- Troll HP: 18 - 3 = 15/18
- Challenge effect: Troll must attack Fighter when possible

Troll Response (must attack Fighter per Challenge):
- Melee Attack vs Fighter: rolls 6
- Exploding die! Rolls again: gets 4
- Total: 6 + 4 = 10 damage (plus Strong bonus +1 = 11 total)
- Fighter Defence: rolls 5, blocks 5 damage
- Fighter takes: 11 - 5 = 6 damage
- Fighter HP: 10 - 6 = 4/10 (DANGER ZONE)

Fighter Situation: Low HP, must act carefully next round

ROUND 2:

Fighter Action:
- Decides: use Focus on weapon for guaranteed 6 damage
- Combat Master triggers: rolls d6 to add = gets 3
- Total damage: 6 + 3 = 9 damage
- Troll Defence: rolls 1, blocks 1 damage
- Troll takes: 9 - 1 = 8 damage
- Troll HP: 15 - 8 = 7/18
- Focus used up (1 per encounter with Focus x1 weapon)

Troll Attack:
- Forced to attack Fighter (Challenge still active)
- Rolls 4 + Strong = 5 damage
- Fighter Defence: rolls 6! Gets Heroic Reroll? (rolls 3, no extra reroll)
- Fighter blocks 6, takes 0 damage
- Fighter HP: 4/10 (still alive)

ROUND 3:

Fighter Action:
- Makes normal Melee Attack: rolls 4
- Troll Defence: rolls 2, takes 2 damage
- Troll HP: 7 - 2 = 5/18

Troll Attack:
- Rolls 3 + Strong = 4 damage
- Fighter Defence: rolls 2, takes 2 damage
- Fighter HP: 4 - 2 = 2/10 (critical)

Fighter Reassessment:
- Fighter at 2/10 HP
- Troll at 5/18 HP
- Fighter could: Full Defence, Retreat, or Continue Fighting
- Decides: Full Defence next round to block more damage
- Could also use Heroic Reroll if available for big attack

ROUND 4:

Fighter Action:
- Uses Full Defence (foregos attack)
- Now re-rolls all Defence Actions, taking best result

Troll Attack:
- Rolls 5 + Strong = 6 damage
- Fighter Defence: rolls 4, re-rolls (Full Defence), gets 6
- Takes best: 6, blocks all 6 damage
- Fighter takes: 0 damage
- Fighter HP: 2/10 (still standing)

[Battle continues with Fighter Full Defence protecting self...]

KEY TAKEAWAYS FOR FIGHTER:
‚úì Challenge controls enemy actions

### CLERIC PROFILE

**Core Stats:**
- HP: 8 (mid-range)
- CS: d6-1 (slight combat penalty)
- Melee: Mace, Chain Mail
- Role: Support, healing, undead damage

**Combat Strengths:**
1. **Healing in Combat:** Holy Light heals d6-1 HP, re-roll taking best, minimum 1
2. **Area Healing:** Holy Light heals ALL friendly targets in area
3. **Undead Damage:** Holy Smite does d6-1 damage to undead, no Defence allowed
4. **Limited Spells:** 2 Holy Light OR 2 Holy Smite OR 1 of each per encounter
5. **Righteous Insight:** Re-roll any wisdom task, take best
6. **Bind Wounds:** After encounters, heal 2 HP to all (vs Bind Wounds standard 1 HP)

**Combat Weaknesses:**
1. **CS d6-1:** Lowest attack rolls except special cases
2. **Limited Spell Uses:** Only 2 spell uses per encounter total
3. **Lower HP:** 8 HP means can't sustain lots of damage
4. **Melee Penalty:** Can't take heavy armor (chain mail only)
5. **Area Healing:** Must be strategic about range/positioning
6. **No Escape:** Healing others while injured puts self at risk

**Optimal Combat Positioning:**
- Mid-rank (not front, not completely back)
- In hearing range of entire party (area healing)
- Behind primary tank (Fighter)
- Position to see all allies
- Near allies who might need emergency healing

**Resource Management:**
- 2 holy spells total per encounter (critical resource)
- Decide: healing priority or damage priority
- Heroic Reroll extends spells (gets +1 extra spell)
- After-battle Bind Wounds heals 2 HP (standard is 1)

**Cleric Combat Strategy:**

**Against Undead (Optimal Cleric Scenario):**
1. Use Holy Smite for no-Defence damage (powerful!)
2. Holy Smite does d6-1 damage, re-roll taking best
3. Average 3 damage per Holy Smite with no Defence roll
4. 2 Holy Smites per encounter = 6 damage average to undead
5. Combine with allies attacking for massive damage

**Against Mixed Combat (Multiple Allies Damaged):**
1. Prioritize: Who needs healing most?
2. Holy Light hits all friendly targets in area
3. Heals d6-1, re-roll taking best (min 1)
4. Average 3-4 HP per Holy Light to each target
5. Use Holy Light first (support role primary)
6. Save Holy Smite for undead if present

**Against Single Powerful Enemy:**
1. Role: Support for primary tank (usually Fighter)
2. Holy Light to keep tank alive
3. Mace attacks to contribute damage (CS d6-1 still does damage)
4. Decide: spend spell on damage or healing?
5. If allies healthy: use Holy Smite (damage)
6. If allies hurt: use Holy Light (healing)

```

### THIEF PROFILE

**Core Stats:**
- HP: 6 (lowest starting HP)
- CS: d6-1 (melee/ranged penalty)
- Melee/Ranged: Dagger and Bow, Leather armor
- Role: Striker, controller, repositioner

**Combat Strengths:**
1. **Flank:** Attack same enemy as ally = roll twice, take best result
2. **Backstab:** Unaware enemy, successful agility task = 2 CS d6 attacks combined
3. **Nimble:** Re-roll any agility task, take best result
4. **Trap Finding/Disarming:** Detect and remove traps
5. **High Mobility:** Agility-based repositioning
6. **Surprise Attacks:** Backstab can auto-surprise enemies

**Combat Weaknesses:**
1. **Lowest HP:** 6 HP means dies fastest
2. **CS d6-1:** Both melee and ranged attacks weak
3. **Fragile:** Can't take hits, must avoid damage
4. **Limited Armor:** Leather only (no heavy protection)
5. **Needs Positioning:** Flank requires ally adjacent
6. **Backstab Situational:** Requires unaware target

**Optimal Combat Positioning:**
- Side-by-side with Tank (Fighter) for Flank attacks
- Mobile (not locked in place)
- Multiple escape routes available
- Range: next to attacking ally (Flank range)
- Cover/concealment for survivability
- NOT in full front-line combat

**Resource Management:**
- No spells or abilities with limited uses
- Depends on tactical positioning
- Heroic Rerolls on attack rolls critical (low CS d6-1)
- Can Flank unlimited times (no limit per encounter)

**Thief Combat Strategy:**

**Against Single Strong Enemy (Tank is Fighting):**
1. Position adjacent to Tank
2. Use Flank attacks (roll twice, take best)
3. With Nimble bonus on agility: attack rolls better
4. Let Tank get hit, Thief stays at range
5. Multiple allies flanking = enemies outnumbered tactically
6. Use repositioning to avoid focused enemy attacks

**Against Multiple Weak Enemies:**
1. Pick one to focus (like Goblin Pack)
2. Flank if ally is also attacking it
3. Move away if enemy approaches alone
4. Use bow for ranged attacks when alone
5. Reposition between rounds to maintain Flank
6. Mobility is advantage here

**Backstab Scenario (Pre-Combat Surprise):**
1. If unaware of Thief and succeeds agility task
2. Makes 2 CS d6 Melee attacks, combines damage
3. With Nimble re-roll on agility task: higher success
4. Can auto-surprise target (PM discretion)
5. Devastating opening attack before real combat
6. After backstab: real combat begins

### WIZARD PROFILE

**Core Stats:**
- HP: 4 (LOWEST - most fragile)
- CS: d6-2 (severe combat penalty)
- Melee/Ranged: Staff of magic missiles, Robes
- Role: Area damage, control, utility

**Combat Strengths:**
1. **Combat Magic:** 2 spells per encounter from Magic Spell List
2. **Spell Variety:** Multiple spell options (damage, control, healing, summoning)
3. **Area Damage:** Spells hit multiple targets (Rain of Fire, Confusion)
4. **Summon Monster:** Control monster for 3-6 rounds
5. **No Combat Melee:** Don't need to fight, stay at range
6. **Heroic Reroll Extension:** Can cast additional spells with Rerolls

**Combat Weaknesses:**
1. **LOWEST HP:** 4 HP (dies in 1-2 solid hits)
2. **LOWEST CS:** d6-2 (staff attacks do almost no damage)
3. **Limited Spells:** Only 2 per encounter (precious resource)
4. **Fragile:** Can't take any hits at all
5. **No Defense:** Robes provide no armor bonus
6. **Range-Dependent:** Must stay far away or die

**Optimal Combat Positioning:**
- 40+ feet from enemies (maximum spell range)
- Behind Tank (Fighter protecting)
- Cover/concealment if possible
- Clear line of sight to targets
- Escape route planned
- NOT in melee range under any circumstances

**Resource Management:**
- 2 spells per encounter (PRECIOUS)
- Each spell is combat action
- Can't move AND cast (usually move OR cast)
- Heroic Rerolls unlock additional spells
- After 2 spells: limited to staff attacks (CS d6-2, ~1 damage per hit)

**Wizard Combat Strategy:**

**Against Single Powerful Enemy (Troll, Minotaur):**
1. **Spell #1 Strategy:** Area damage if multiple enemies (Rain of Fire), or Control (Confusion) if dangerous
2. Alternative: Summon Monster to create ally that fights while Wizard stays back
3. Position 40+ feet away
4. Let Tank (Fighter) engage main enemy
5. Wizard attacks with summoned ally or spells
6. If Tank is losing: use Holy Light spell? NO (Wizard doesn't have it)
7. Use second spell wisely: more damage or strategic advantage?

**Against Multiple Weak Enemies (Goblin Pack):**
1. **Perfect Wizard Scenario:** Area spells shine here
2. Rain of Fire hits up to 4 targets close together
3. One Rain of Fire vs Goblin Pack = potentially kills multiple goblins
4. Confusion spell stops enemies from attacking (no movement, no attacks)
5. Use both spells to control/damage pack
6. Summoned monster fights from front

### BARD PROFILE

**Core Stats:**
- HP: 6 (low, like Thief)
- CS: d6-2 (low, like Wizard staff attacks)
- Melee/Ranged: Any 1-handed weapon, bow, Leather/Padded armor
- Role: Support, buffs/debuffs, CC

**Combat Strengths:**
1. **Bardic Songs:** Magical songs that buff allies/debuff enemies
2. **Hymn of the Mighty:** +1 damage all allies
3. **Lay of Deflection:** -1 damage all allies (damage mitigation)
4. **Melody of Madness:** Confuse enemies (3 rounds duration)
5. **Song of Mending:** Heal all allies (3 rounds to activate)
6. **Charming:** Re-roll any charisma task, take best

**Combat Weaknesses:**
1. **CS d6-2:** Weak melee/ranged attacks (like Wizard)
2. **Low HP:** 6 HP (dies quickly)
3. **Song Setup Time:** 3 rounds minimum before new song
4. **Limited Armor:** Leather/Padded only
5. **Performance-Based:** Needs to be heard (range matters)
6. **No Instant Effects:** Spells need 3 rounds to finish

**Optimal Combat Positioning:**
- Mid-rank (behind Tank, not hiding)
- Hearing range of all allies (songs affect all in range)
- Not engaged in melee if possible
- Can move while singing
- Clear voice projection area

**Resource Management:**
- Unlimited songs (3 combat rounds minimum setup)
- Each round must continue singing (can take other actions)
- Switch songs only after 3 rounds
- No daily limits on songs
- Heroic Rerolls don't extend songs

**Bard Combat Strategy:**

**Buff Scenario (Party vs Strong Enemy):**
1. Cast Hymn of the Mighty (start of combat)
2. Round 1-3: Singing (+1 damage all allies)
3. Round 3: All allies hit +1 damage per attack
4. Fighter with +1 damage = significant boost
5. Wizard spells unaffected by damage buff
6. Stacks with Lay of Deflection if running both

**Defense Scenario (Party Taking Damage):**
1. Start Lay of Deflection song
2. Rounds 1-3: All allies take -1 damage
3. Fighter taking less damage = survives longer
4. Healing support from Cleric still works
5. Stacks with offense buffs

**Crowd Control Scenario (Multiple Enemies):**
1. Cast Melody of Madness
2. Enemies make tough task roll or become confused
3. Confused = no movement, no attacks (3 rounds)
4. Can still use Defence Actions
5. Powerful crowd control without damage
6. Gives allies time to reposition/heal

### RANGER PROFILE

**Core Stats:**
- HP: 10 (tied for highest, like Fighter)
- CS: d6-1 (melee and ranged penalty, like Thief/Cleric)
- Melee/Ranged: Sword and Long Bow, Leather armor
- Role: Ranged striker, environmental specialist, support

**Combat Strengths:**
1. **Ranged Mastery:** Either 1 exploding ranged attack OR 2 ranged attacks at different targets
2. **Alert:** Party rerolls surprise, Ranger rerolls initiative in woodland/cavern
3. **Survivalist:** Non-combat tasks re-roll in wilderness/caverns
4. **Tied Highest HP:** 10 HP (like Fighter)
5. **Sword and Bow:** Versatile combat options
6. **Woodland Bond:** Strong in environmental play

**Combat Weaknesses:**
1. **CS d6-1:** Both melee and ranged attacks weak
2. **Conditional Abilities:** Only work in woodland/cavern (not in dungeon)
3. **No Healing:** Can't support allies
4. **No Area Spells:** Single target or split attacks
5. **No Armor Bonus:** Leather only
6. **Niche Role:** Weaker in stone dungeons (common location)

**Optimal Combat Positioning:**
- Ranged distance (not adjacent)
- On high ground if possible
- Behind Tank for protection
- Can see targets clearly
- Have movement options

**Resource Management:**
- Unlimited ranged attacks
- Choose: 1 exploding OR 2 attacks per round
- Exploding attacks risk missing (single target, needs 6)
- 2 attacks spread damage (multiple targets or confirmed damage)
- No spell slots or limited abilities

**Ranger Combat Strategy:**

**In Woodland/Cavern (Optimal):**
1. Reroll initiative (Ranger has advantage)
2. Survivalist tasks have reroll (non-combat perks)
3. Alert party to dangers
4. Use ranged superiority

**Single Strong Target (Troll):**
1. Use exploding ranged attack (risk high reward)
2. Roll: need 6 to explode, then add next d6
3. Against Troll CS d6: likely blocked (Troll blocks d6 = average 3.5)
4. Successful explosion deals significant damage
5. Combine with Fighter's attacks

**Multiple Targets (Goblin Pack):**
1. Use 2 ranged attacks option
2. Split attacks between two different goblins
3. Targets each reroll Defence Actions (Ranger's weakness)
4. Not ideal for Ranger (Wizard's area spells better)
5. Better than 1 exploding attack against multiple

- **Synergy:** Cleric heals Fighter, Fighter takes all damage
- **Strength:** Fighter survives longer, Cleric support role
- **Weakness:** If Cleric dies, Fighter alone (can happen)

- **Synergy:** Thief Flanks with Fighter (doubled rolls)
- **Strength:** Both maximize damage, crowd control
- **Weakness:** Both melee-based, vulnerable to ranged
- **Example:** Fighter and Thief kill goblins in 1-2 rounds

### Wizard + Fighter
- **Synergy:** Wizard area spells, Fighter protects Wizard
- **Strength:** Wizard doesn't die, massive area damage
- **Weakness:** If Fighter distracted, Wizard vulnerable
- **Example:** Rain of Fire kills goblins, Fighter protects

### Cleric + Wizard
- **Synergy:** Cleric heals Wizard (lowest HP), Wizard does area damage
- **Strength:** Support + magic = powerful combo
- **Weakness:** Both have low HP, front-line vulnerable
- **Example:** Holy Light heals Wizard, Rain of Fire wins battle

### Full Party
- **Synergy:** Every role covered (Tank, Support, Damage, Control)
- **Strength:** Can handle any encounter
- **Weakness:** Coordination required
- **Example:** Fighter tanks, Cleric heals, Wizard area, Thief flanks

- Wizard MVP of encounter
- Extremely favorable for Wizard

- Holy Smite does d6-1 damage (no Defence allowed!)

- Cleric strong vs undead
